ABOUT

Valentine’s Day, on February 14, symbolizes love, with Cupid as its icon. In Roman mythology, Cupid, the son of Venus, uses magical arrows to make people fall in love. A famous tale tells of Cupid and Psyche, a mortal whose beauty rivaled Venus. Despite trials, their love endured, and Psyche gained immortality, symbolizing true love’s power. Cupid remains a symbol of romance and passion today.
